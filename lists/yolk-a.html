  1. Rent-a-Pet Service:        
  • Customer segment: people not ready to commit to the responsibilities of full-time pet ownership.
  • value proposition: have a ‘pet’ for a day, or several, for a fraction of the cost and time commitment that an actual pet would entail.

  • revenue streams: rental income and ‘placement’ fees for pet that owners decide to adopt permanently.
  • key resources: ‘storage’-- kennel to house and take care of pets while not rented. online platform for advertisement and animal display & description, rental booking. drop-off/pick-up locations around the city.

  •  cost structure: costs include location/kennel rental; other pet products/caretaking (food, medication, vaccinations, neutering, etc); service advertisement; logistics for pet drop-off/pick up.
