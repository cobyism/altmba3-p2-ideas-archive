  1. A bicycle co-op store where the biking community interacts including volunteers teaching customers how to fix their bikes on-site, recycling parts, offer discounted parts and organizing community events.
  • Value prop: We help customers help themselves by teaching them how to fix and maintain their bikes instead of doing it for them and resell used bicycle parts
  • Customer segment: We attract cyclists who want to learn how to service their own bikes or receive tune-ups at minimal cost.
  • Channel: We connect with our customers primarily through online channels, including local search results and our website, but word of mouth also drives a lot of foot traffic into our local stores.
  • Revenue: Our revenue comes from subscription fees, events, and resell of used bicycle parts.
  1. “DIY Handyman” service where a trained professional will come to your home and teach you how to perform the task you’re looking to do.
  • Value prop: We help homeowners and renters transition from relying on contractors for small jobs to teaching them how to do the small jobs themselves.
  • Customer segment: We target budget-minded homeowners and renters who are interested in becoming self-sufficient.
  • Channel: Our customers find us through a variety of online channels, including online local search, where we run a variety of ad campaigns, and our online YouTube channel, where our team walks people through a variety of common household repairs.
  • Revenue: We charge our customers a variable usage fee depending on the work they need done.
  1. App where you can have almost anything delivered. Send out a request, somebody agrees to it and delivers it to you.

  • Customer segment: We’re a multi-sided platform that primarily targets customers who want items delivered to where they are, but we also work hard to attract and grow our network of delivery drivers.
  • Channel: Our customers find us through the usual online channels, including online search, but we also rely heavily on word of mouth from our satisfied users and our network of delivery drivers. Users download our app from the Apple App Store and the Google Play store. Our drivers find us through our website and the jobs ads we post on Indeed.com.
  • Revenue: Our customers pay a variable usage fee depending on the size and complexity of their request.
