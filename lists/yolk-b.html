  55. App/Website for Promoting Healthy Lifestyle
    Changes in lifestyle can help address widespread chronic diseases. Create a software system where one can submit one’s picture and see it ‘age’ 5-10-15-20 years from now based on what lifestyle they choose - exercise, no exercise, vegetarian, non-vegetarian, smoking, non-smoking, alcohol, no alcohol, consume 100-200-500 calories extra/less every day, etc. (age progression software already exists and one can borrow from those ideas). This will help people see visually what the impact of their action will lead to.

    Sell: Advice/articles/links/ads
    How it helps: people can clearly visualize impact of their choices and make a better decision
