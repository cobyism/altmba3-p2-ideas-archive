Ashley Hathaway 

  1. A business that specializes in gear for athletes who do vigorous exercises on their own and have a hard time keeping their headphones in. Product would be headphones that pass the burpee test (i.e.- you can do 10 burpees without having your headphones fall out)
  2. A business that specializes in gear for female athletes who do vigorous exercises on their own and have a hard time finding gear that meets their needs. Product idea is headphones that are fit especially for smaller ears, gloves that help women build up grip strength and develop callouses (it’s a weird group trust me).
  3.  A business that specializes in helping weightlifting athletes train safely without a coach on site. Product idea is an app that takes pictures or video of your squat and checks your form.
  4. A business that specializes in uniting high intensity athletes who workout on their own. Product idea is a video app that allows you to do crossfit/hiit style workouts together in a group video setting forum. Athletes can still workout independently but have the energy of a group session.
  5. A business that focuses on athlete rehabilitation by providing video services to athletes to put them in touch with a coach or sports medicine specialist that can provide training and video training sessions that improves form remotely.
  6. A business that focuses on athlete rehabilitation by providing specialized training kits and videos to rehabilitate athletes remotely. The mission of the company is to keep athletes from getting injured regardless of location.
  7. A business that specializes in connecting athletes to other athletes that workout in similar ways. Maybe there’s someone on the other bench press that is doing the same round of training as you. Post your workouts for your gym and find swole mates.
  8. A business that specializes in active saving. Product is an app that notifies you when you enter a store what you spent the last time you were there, your budget for the month of that kind of business (i.e.- go into a coffee shop and get a notification about your coffee budget. go into a grocery store get info about your grocery budget)
  9. An athletic store that provides training for their products in the store for people that are new to exercising that aims to teach people how to train better. The mission is to get people to buy equipment they’ll actually use.
  10. An athletic site that allows girls looking for sports teams to find others in their area interested in the same sports by connecting people based on age, location, and interest. The mission is to increase girls self esteem through sport and connecting with other girls interested in the same things they are.
  11. A business that aims to provide people with coupons for things they actually care about. They target your purchase history and then surface coupons from retailers to alert consumers to these coupons in store. The mission is to create a better relationship w/ retailer & consumer by increasing repeat visits through incentives.
  12. A business that provides personal money saving services based on location with the mission to keep people aware of their spending based on where they travel. The company uses geolocation as well as your personal spending habits to learn how to optimize your budget. The rev stream is through data collection of demographics and consumer spending sold to stores to better engage their customers.
  13. A business that provides personal money services to consumers and b2b with the mission to provide granular-level detail about their spending patterns using machine learning. The mission is to track every penny and keep consumers who are unaware of their spending in check.
  14. A business that aims to provide small businesses clarity into every penny they spend through the use of OCR’s of receipts. Any business can consolidate digital receipts as well as itemized information based on their purchases and then quickly allocate personal/business spending per item. The mission is to give new business owners confidence when purchasing and managing their assets & liabilities.
  15. A business that aims to help small business owners find ways to save money by ensuring they deduct items correctly and adhere to the best tax code possible. The company provides software to streamline multiple expense streams into a single location. The user is then notified of potential purchases that can be deducted or ways to file taxes better. The mission is to give insurance companies confidence in taxes during their first years of business.
  16. A business whose mission is to never make consumers go to the store again. By tracking consumer purchase history the product learns time frame and frequency of specific item purchases and then ships them to consumers before they need them. One rev stream is through affiliate marketing through sites like Instacart or Amazon.
  17. A business whose mission is to alert you to local produce in your area that you may not have known about. Did you know there’s a local garden 3 blocks over that has the most amazing squash? Neither did I. The product unites local growers and consumers who are interested in sustainable agriculture and supporting local communities.
  18. A business that ensures you never have an embarrassing photo online. Their mission is to provide individuals a way to search for their images and through the use of image recognition find potential matches they didn’t know existed.
  19. A business whose mission is to never let international travellers rack up crazy cell fees by alerting them to local wifi spots they can connect to for free. Rev stream is through local retailers who make their wifi network known through location services which a consumer is notified for free using their phone.
  20. A business whose mission is to never let travlers suffer jet lag ever again. Through the use of time zone information and your normal circadian rhythm pulled from Fitbit, sleep monitoring software, MyFitnessPal, etc. consumers are alerted when they should try and nap, when they should maybe have a coffee, when they should exercise, etc.
